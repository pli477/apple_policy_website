Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2024

1 Information We Collect

When you use our App, we may process the following information:

  • Content you provide: Images or videos you upload for style transformation.
  • Device and log data: Device model, operating system version, crash logs, and error codes to help us troubleshoot and improve stability.

We do not collect personally identifiable information (such as your real name, ID, phone number, or address).

2 How We Use Your Information

  • One-time processing: Your uploaded content is converted to base64 format and securely transmitted to third-party AI models (e.g., Google Gemini) strictly for generating the requested output.
  • No storage: We do not store your uploaded images, videos, or generated results on our servers.
  • User control: You are responsible for downloading and saving the generated result. If you do not download it, it may not be retrievable later.
  • Technical logs: We only keep minimal call records (such as task ID, timestamp, status codes) for service monitoring. These logs contain no original content.

3 Sharing With Third Parties

To provide the service, your content may be encrypted and transmitted to third-party providers:

  • Google Gemini – image/video understanding and generation
  • OpenAI – image/video understanding and generation

We ensure third-party services only process your data as needed for functionality.

We do not sell, rent, or otherwise share your information with advertisers or unrelated third parties, except:

  • As required by law, regulation, or valid legal process.
  • With your explicit consent.

4 Data Storage and Deletion

  • Immediate deletion: Uploaded content is not stored on our servers and is removed automatically once the task is completed.
  • No server backup: If you do not download the result, it cannot be recovered.
  • User request: You may contact us via Apple to request deletion of technical call logs, which we will handle within a reasonable time.

5 Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with content, please contact us so we can delete it.

6 Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access or request deletion of data related to you.
  • Withdraw permissions (e.g., camera or photo library access) at any time.

7 Data Security

We apply industry-standard measures to protect information during transfer, including encryption and access controls.

8 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be notified within the App or via other reasonable means before taking effect.
